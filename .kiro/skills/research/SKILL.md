# Research Workflow

Execute research workflow based on research type:

1. Identify research type (market, competitive, domain, technical)
2. Load appropriate workflow from `_bmad/bmm/workflows/1-analysis/research/`
3. Follow research methodology in the selected workflow
4. Gather and analyze relevant information
5. Document research findings and insights
6. Save research outputs to project documentation
